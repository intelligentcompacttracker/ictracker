About us

We are dedicated to providing the best tracking solutions to keep your family safe and secure. The Intelligent Compact Tracker is a GPS tracking device that aims to provide safety and security for children and bike owners. The device is compact, affordable, and reliable, with features such as real-time tracking, geofencing, and emergency alerts. The target customers are parents with school-going children, schools, bike owners, and bike rental companies. The marketing strategy includes digital marketing, partnerships, promotions, and public relations. The company plans to launch the device within a year, followed by product enhancements and market expansion. The team consists of a founder, co-founder, developers, finance manager, and social media marketer. The financial model includes direct sales, subscription plans, and service fees, with top cash burn reasons being research and development, marketing and sales, operations and infrastructure, market expansion, and customer acquisition and retention.
